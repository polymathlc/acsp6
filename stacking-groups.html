Stacking Groups — Country X Coin Problem

In Country X, the height of 6 ten-cent coins equals the height of 5 twenty-cent coins. Add one group at a time and watch both stacks grow at the same rate until the two stacks together are worth $88.

5 × $0.20 = $1.00
